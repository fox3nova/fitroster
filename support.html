FAQ

Common support questions.

Do I need a subscription?

No. FitRoster presents its core training workflow without requiring a recurring subscription.

Where is the usage guide?

The current Traditional Chinese PDF is available from this support page and the product page. English and Simplified Chinese guide slots are reserved for future updates.

What should I include for sync or backup questions?

Send the app version, device model, iOS version, the role you use, and whether the question is about iCloud sync, coach-trainee sharing, or backup files.

Will more languages be supported?

Yes. The release site and guide library are structured for more language versions, and additional guides will be added progressively.
